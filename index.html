What is ransomware and why is it a threat to GCC countries? +

Ransomware is malicious software that encrypts victims' data and demands payment for decryption. GCC countries are prime targets due to their wealth, rapid digitalization, and critical infrastructure. Saudi Arabia and UAE have seen over 180 confirmed ransomware victims in the past two years.

How does GulfCyber.io collect threat intelligence? +

We aggregate data from multiple sources including dark web ransomware leak sites (accessed via Tor), public threat intelligence feeds, and security researcher disclosures. Our automated systems update every 6 hours to provide near real-time visibility.

Which industries are most targeted in Saudi Arabia and UAE? +

The most targeted sectors include Technology, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Finance, Government, Construction, and Energy. Notable Saudi victims include healthcare providers, automotive companies, and government-linked entities. UAE targets include real estate, professional services, and government organizations.
